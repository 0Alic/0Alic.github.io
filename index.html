Andrea Lisi was initially created without any class.

After defeating his first final boss, the Bachelorlich, he started a new journey to look for a prestigious loot: the "Master Scroll of Computers", also known as MSc.
That quest needed much effort, healing potions and even a re-spawn, but Andrea Lisi somehow made it.
